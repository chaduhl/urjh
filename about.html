About us

Our Story

​The Undergraduate Research Journal for the Humanities is a student-run journal for University of Kansas Humanities students. Its primary goal is to provide a venue for undergraduates to publish their research in the Humanities. Its second goal is to promote the viability and brilliance of the Humanities. It is designed to be a place where undergraduates can share their research. It was developed in 2015 by KU students and will publish its first issue in September 2016.

The current team is led by Editor-in-Chief Savannah Pine.

Check us out on Facebook under Undergraduate Research Journal for the Humanities! Check us out on Twitter (@urjh_ku)!
